Who We Are

We're a collective of passionate explorers, storytellers, and adventure seekers dedicated to bringing you authentic travel experiences.

Founded in 2015 by Sarah Johnson, Mayor began as a personal travel blog documenting backpacking adventures across Southeast Asia. What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted resource for thousands of travelers worldwide.

Our mission is simple: to inspire responsible travel and help adventurers discover the world's hidden gems while preserving their natural beauty for future generations.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every adventure we share

Sustainable Travel

We promote eco-friendly practices and support local communities to ensure our adventures leave positive impacts on the places we visit.

Authentic Experiences

We go beyond tourist spots to bring you genuine cultural encounters and off-the-beaten-path discoveries that most travelers miss.

Community First

We believe travel should benefit local populations as much as travelers, fostering mutual understanding and economic support.
